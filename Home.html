Welcome in the World of HajimeNoIppo!


Step into the world of Hajime no Ippo, where heart, determination, and passion collide in the electrifying sport of boxing! Meet Ippo Makunouchi, a kind-hearted but shy high schooler whose life takes a thrilling turn when he discovers his hidden strength in the ring. With the guidance of the lively and fearless Takamura Mamoru, Ippo’s journey is one of personal growth, fierce rivalries, and unforgettable victories. Whether you’re a longtime fan of sports stories or new to anime, Hajime no Ippo welcomes you with open arms to experience a tale of triumph, friendship, and the relentless pursuit of dreams.
